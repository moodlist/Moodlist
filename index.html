Are you very indecisive whenever you have to pick a song? No problem! We are going to do the job for you. We personally created some playlists,touching the most genres possible, for you to choose from. Our main goal is to create a safe space for music lovers around the world, that's why we implemented other features to help you stay in touch with us and with other music lovers like you!

Discover our playlists

We have created a set of different playlists for you to choose from. We tried to give you as much options as we could but we are open to suggestions, so feel free to contact us and tell us which song you think we should add! Our intention was to put both niche and very well known songs in each of our playlists in order to give you the possibility to also discover some new tunes.

Song of the week

We want you to be as involved as possible! Every week we will post some polls on our social media accounts and you get to decide which song will be crowned as the absolute bop of the week. Polls will close on Friday and you will see the result posted on Saturday!


Who are we?

Hi everyone! We are Irene and Marta. We study science of communication at the University of Bologna. Although we live in Italy, we have decided to use English language to include everyone!
We have created this site for music lovers like us by personally selecting the songs in the various playlists.
We hope to widen the community as much as possibile and that the opinions and musical tastes of users can allow others to re-evaluate some musical genres or to discover new ones.
We invite you to share with us and with other users your experiences related to music, your personal tastes and your opinions to allow us to get to know each other better and let someone open their musical horizon.
