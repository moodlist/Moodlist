The Red Hot Chilli Peppers album Unlimited Love, out April first 2022, can’t be defined as one of their best albums although I’ve been waiting for it for sone years now. You can perceive an influence from the previous albums which leaves the listener a certain feeling of nostalgia. The song I liked the most was Black Summer, the one they released to promote the album.

The Linkin Park album “Meteora”, out in 2003, is the second album published by the American band and it is considered their best work up to date. The album is inscribable in the nu metal genre and mixes marvellously heavy metal, rap, hip hop and electronic elements. My favourite song from the album is Numb.

@Berserk988 via Twitter

My favourite singer is Eros Ramazzotti. Why him? Well I've been listening to his music since I was a child, I danced and sang his songs at the top of my lungs. In my opinion, the true essence of his singing career is to be found at the beginning of his journey. The song that made Eros famous was "Terra Promessa", written in 1984, and I think that thanks to this tune you can truly understand the energy in his voice and in his way of making music.

@light_berta via Instagram

I've been listening to Akaseven lately because I empathize a lot with the lyrics of his songs. My favourite song right now is "Perfetta così", he sang it at the Festival di Sanremo this year. This song has a very special meaning to me: acceptance of oneself both physically and temperamentally, unluckily today is very difficult to do so.
